======

I am currently a Ph.D. student and graduate student researcher at the University of California, Irvine. I am a member of the [Autonomous and Intelligent Cyber-Physical Systems (AICPS) lab](https://aicps.eng.uci.edu/), which is led by Professor Al Faruque. My research is primarily focused on the security of Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) and Embedded Systems. More specifically, I am interested in sensor security for CPS and binary analysis for embedded systems. I also have experience in side-channel analysis, applied machine learning for security, PLC security, and PLC binary analysis.

During the summer of 2021, I had the privilege of interning at [Siemens Technology](https://www.siemens.com/global/en.html) as a Ph.D. intern. There, I contributed to an industrial research project on engineering software for micro-patch generation. My work was focused on utilizing a machine learning-based approach for binary characterization and patch generation.

Before starting my Ph.D. at UCI, I obtained my BSc degree in Electrical Engineering from the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology. During my undergrad, I had internship experiences at [Lablup Inc.](https://www.lablup.com/) and [KAIST Cyber Security Research Center (CSRC)](https://csrc.kaist.ac.kr/).
